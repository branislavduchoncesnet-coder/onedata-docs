### To use Onedata via e-INFRA CZ, you need:

#### 1. An e-INFRA CZ Account
To be able to use Onedata, you need to __be part of e-infra organization__. If you are not already, you can request access [here](https://docs.account.e-infra.cz/en/docs/access/account).

#### 2. Access to the Onedata Portal
Onedata offers several ways to access your data, so you can choose whatever fits your workflow best:

#### 🌐 Web Portal
The easiest way to get started. Access your files through a browser — no installation required.
- URL: [https://onedata.e-infra.cz](https://onedata.e-infra.cz)
- Best for: browsing files, managing shares, inviting collaborators, and basic uploads/downloads

#### 🖥️ Oneclient
Mount your Onedata Space as a local directory on your Linux computer or HPC cluster. Once mounted, you can work with files as if they were on your local disk.
- Best for: command-line workflows, HPC jobs, scripting, and working with large datasets
- Requires: Linux, installation of the `oneclient` package
More info about setp of client is [here](/docs/how-to/how-to-use-client)

#### 3. A Space to Work In
Before you can start uploading, sharing and colaborating, you'll need __data Space__.
